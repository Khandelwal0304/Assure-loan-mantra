Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum credit score required for a loan?

Generally, a credit score of 650 or above is preferred. However, we work with various banks and NBFCs that have different requirements. Some lenders may approve loans even with a score of 600+.

How long does it take to get loan approval?

Loan approval typically takes 24-48 hours after submission of all required documents. Some loans may be approved faster, especially personal loans which can be approved within 24 hours.

Is there any processing fee?

Many of our partner banks offer zero processing fee during promotional periods. Processing fees, when applicable, typically range from 0.5% to 2% of the loan amount depending on the lender and loan type.

Can I check my loan application status online?

Yes, you can track your loan application status through our partner portal or by logging into your account. You'll also receive regular updates via SMS and email at each stage of the process.

How do I become a loan partner?

You can register as a loan partner by filling out our partner registration form. Once approved, you'll get access to our partner dashboard, training materials, and can start earning commissions by referring customers.
